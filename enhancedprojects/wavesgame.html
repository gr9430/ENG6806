The Waves Forget the Structure

My work as an interdisciplinary scholar-practitioner lies at the intersection of creative practice, critical making, and decolonial inquiry. Through this ePortfolio, I reflect on my experiences in ENG 6806: Humanities in the Age of AI, where I have explored generative AI, digital storytelling, and critical media analysis. Throughout this course, I have gained invaluable insights into my dual identity as an academic and creative, discovering how to blend these aspects seamlessly to create meaningful work.
